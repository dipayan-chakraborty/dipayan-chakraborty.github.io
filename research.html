Research

Research Areas

Absolute DEA Cross-Section Measurements

Development of experimental techniques for measuring absolute dissociative electron attachment cross-sections in gas-phase molecular systems and understanding electron-driven fragmentation processes.

Velocity Map Imaging (VMI) Studies

Velocity map imaging investigations of low-energy electron induced fragmentation dynamics, kinetic energy distributions, and angular scattering processes in molecules.

Dynamics of Molecular Clusters

Studies of electron interactions with isolated molecular clusters and investigations of fragmentation pathways, structural rearrangements, and collective molecular dynamics.
