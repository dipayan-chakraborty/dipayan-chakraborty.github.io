Velocity Map Imaging (VMI) Studies

Overview

This research focuses on the development and application of velocity map imaging (VMI) spectroscopy for investigating low-energy electron induced molecular fragmentation dynamics, kinetic energy distributions, and angular scattering processes in molecules.
